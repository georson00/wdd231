Nelka Fitness Site Plan

1. Site Name

Nelka Fitness - The name reflects a unique, personal, and professional fitness brand. "Nelka" blends individuality with a sense of community, while "Fitness" directly expresses the site's focus on health and wellness.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of Nelka Fitness is to provide fitness enthusiasts, beginners, and anyone on a wellness journey with helpful resources. This includes personalized workout plans, health and nutrition tips, motivational articles, and a supportive online community.

3. Scenarios

  • How can I find an easy workout routine to follow at home without equipment?
  • What tips does Nelka Fitness have for staying motivated with my fitness goals?

4. Color Schema

  • Primary Color – #ff3c38 (Bright Red) Used for headers, links, and call-to-action buttons to promote calmness and energy.
  • Accent Color – #ffc107 (Amber) Used for highlights, icons, and action prompts to inspire action and enthusiasm.
  • Secondary Color– #2e2e2e (Charcoal) Used for a second option for headers, links and texts.
  • Contrast Color #ffffff– (white) Used for texts.

5. Color Schema Use in Document

This site plan uses the chosen colors in the headings and highlights, giving a preview of the visual branding.

6. Typography

The primary font used will be Arial, a clean and professional sans-serif typeface for all content. It ensures readability on all devices. Headers will be bold and slightly larger for emphasis.
